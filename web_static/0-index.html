Chicken Masala

A delicious and aromatic Indian chicken curry that's easy to make at home.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, marinate the chicken pieces with yogurt, turmeric powder, and salt. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chilies. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  5. Add marinated chicken along with the marinade. Cook until the chicken is tender and the oil separates.
  6. Add coriander powder and garam masala. Stir well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
